Lodging

While visiting Cape Cod, you have a variety of options to stay in. You can stay in any number of hotels, motels, bed and breakfasts.

Check the Cape Cod Chamber of Commerce for all types of accomodations and useful information.

My favorite option is to rent a cottage. I've been doing that for years. You can check vacation rentals by owner or home away websites for all rental information. You can rent a cottage for 1,2, or even 8 people.

Here's a tip! If you visit Cape Cod in the off-season, you can get a huge discount on a cottage.The season usually starts in late June and ends on Labor Day. The website will tell you everything you need to know from size, rates, amenities, etc. Many are fully equipped with kitchens, linens, even pots and pans.
